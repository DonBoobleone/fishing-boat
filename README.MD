# Fishing boat - A Mod for Factorio
This mod adds:
A wooden sailboat. Requires no fuel, catches fish while driving near them.

## Tip
You can catch and release fish by dropping them in water with 'Z'
[Watch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rKjyyxmKQec)
[![Catch and Release Thumbnail](https://img.youtube.com/vi/rKjyyxmKQec/maxresdefault.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rKjyyxmKQec)

## Recommended mods
- Natural Fish Dynamics - Allows fish to reproduce in the wild.
- Cargo Ships - The best ships mod.
- Canal Excavator - Make your own fishing pond
- Planet Pelagos - A nice tropical Planet of fish and coconuts

## Compatibility
- AAI Programmable Vehicles - Allows automation of fishing (not: increases performance cost)
